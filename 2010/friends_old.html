Friends
 
 
 
 
 
 

 

We very much appreciate the help of the Friends of the Wolsey Orchestra, and would also like to thank those who have contributed financially to the orchestra’s success.

If you would like to help secure the future if the orchestra, you might consider helping us with one of our specific expenses.

It may be that you would like to become a Friend of the Wolsey Orchestra. In return for a modest contribution of £25, Friends are entitled to a £2 reduction in ticket prices, a free concert programme and free refreshments in concert intervals.

If you would like to support the orchestra, become a ‘Friend’, or wish to make any other enquiry, please use the feedback form

There is also the opportunity to donate to the orchestra's funds through monthly payroll - please contact us for details
 

Tickets for all our concerts are available from orchestral members, or from the Ipswich Arts and Entertainments Booking Office (01473 433100)

Wolsey Orchestra is a member of the Ipswich Arts Association. To find out more about the Association, its members, events and activities, and to read Images magazine online, visit www.ipswich-arts.org.uk
