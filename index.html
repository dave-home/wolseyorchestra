Our Next Concert

Saturday 11th May at 7:30pm
Burns Recital Hall, Royal Hospital School, Holbrook, IP9 2RX
Conductor - Emma Kent
Guest Leader - Dan Kent
Soloist - Emily Sun
Rossini: Overture - Thieving Magpie
Bruch: Violin Concerto No.1 in G minor, Op.26
Florence Price: Symphony No.1 in E minor

Our final concert of the 2023-24 season will have a distinctly feminine feel to it. The orchestra will be conducted for the first time by a woman, Emma Kent, it is the first time that we will have played a symphony by a woman, Florence Price, and we will be joined in Bruch's ever-popular Violin Concerto by the up-and-coming Australian soloist, Emily Sun. Everyone will be welcome - even men - to what promises to be a stunning summer's evening of music making.

And don't forget to book your tickets early!

Tickets are available now from the Royal Hospital School Box Office
Adults £15, Children / Full-time Students £3
