Our Next Concert

Saturday 4th February 2023 at 7:30pm
The APEX, Bury St. Edmunds
Conductor - Andrew Morley
Leader - Nick Reynolds
Copland: Fanfare for the Common Man
Barber: Adagio for Strings
Sibelius: Karelia Suite, Op.11
Rachmaninov: Symphony No.2 in E minor, Op.28

Our last concert before the global pandemic was in February 2020 at the APEX (less than two months before lockdown hit the UK), and on that occasion the orchestra gave a hugely successful performance of Rachmaninov's Symphony No.3 in A minor. For the orchestra's first post-pandemic visit back to the APEX it is fitting that we are continuing where we left off by performing another of Rachmaninov's famous orchestral works, his Symphony No.2 in E minor, even more so as 2023 is the 150th anniversary of Rachmaninov's birth in 1873!

The first half of the concert will begin with Aaron Copland's supremely evocative Fanfare for the Common Man followed by Samuel Barber's immensely sombre Adagio for Strings - a piece that has been voted as the saddest piece of classical music ever! And then to lift you up before the interval, the orchestra will treat you to probably the most well-known of Sibelius' compositions - his Karelia Suite. This is going to be evening of hugely popular symphonic repertoire and is surely a concert not to be missed!

Tickets will be available soon from the APEX Box Office
1 Charter Square, Bury St. Edmunds IP33 3FD (Tel: 01284 758000).
