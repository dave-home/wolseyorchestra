Our Next Concert

Saturday 13th May 2023 at 7:30pm
Burns Recital Hall, Royal Hospital School, Holbrook, IP9 2RX
Conductor - Richard Laing
Leader - Nick Reynolds
Beethoven: Overture - Fidelio
Elgar: Serenade for Strings, Op.20
Dvořák: Czech Suite, Op.39
Beethoven: Overture - Leonora No.3
Sibelius: Symphony No.5 in E flat major, Op.82

For the last concert of our 2022-2023 season we are returning to the attractive surroundings of the Royal Hospital School to perform a concert of popular orchestral claasics by Beethoven, Elgar, Dvořák and Silelius, including his ever-popular Symphony No.5 in E flat major. And after his superb debut concert in May 2022, we are delighted to welcome back Richard Laing to conduct the orchestra again.

This promises to be a delightful concert for a early summer's evening - so don't forget to book your tickets early!

Tickets are available now from the Royal Hospital School Box Office
