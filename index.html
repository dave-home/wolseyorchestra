Our Next Concert

Saturday 3rd February 2024 at 7:30pm
The APEX, Bury St. Edmunds
Conductor - Andrew Morley
Leader - Nick Reynolds
Rossini: Overture - William Tell
Copland: Four Dance Episodes from Rodeo
Shostakovich: Symphony No.5 in D minor, Op.47

After our hugely sucessful concert at the APEX this time last year, the orchestra, with our conductor Andrew Morley, are delighted to be returning to the APEX to present yet another evening of stunning orchestral music. So why not come and hear the delights of Rossini's overture depicting life in the Swiss Alps and Copland's symphonic suite about life on the ranch in the American Southwest. And after the interval we will treat you to Shostakovich's ever-popular ymphony No.5 which was deemed a "triumphal success" at its premiere in Leningrad in 1937, where it received a standing ovation from both the public and Soviet party officials alike!

This concert promises to be a musical event not to be missed - so don't forget to book your tickets early!

Tickets are available now from the APEX Box Office
1 Charter Square, Bury St. Edmunds IP33 3FD (Tel: 01284 758000).
Adults £17, Children / Full-time Students £5
(a booking fee of £2 is included in these ticket prices)
