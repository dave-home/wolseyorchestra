Our Next Concert

Saturday 12th October at 7:30pm
The Corn Exchange, Ipswich
Conductor - Andrew Morley
Guest Leader - Dan Kent
R Strauss: Fanfare from Also Sprach Zarathustra, Op.30
J Strauss: The Blue Danube
John Williams: Star Wars - Suite for Orchestra
Holst: The Planets Suite, Op.32

For the first concert in our 2024-25 season, we are delighted to be returning to the Ipswich Corn Exchange for the first time since COVID. And our theme for this concert - Space, the final frontier! So come and join us, and our conductor Andrew Morley, as we boldly go and explore the classical music that featured in Stanley Kubrik's ground-breaking film "2001 - a Space Odyssey", the iconic music from John Williams' scores that brought the Star Wars movies to life and Holst's inspirational work, written during WW1, which has captivated concert-going audiences for over a hundred years.

We think this concert will be out of this world - so please book tickets early!

Tickets are available now from the Regent Theatre Box Office,
Adults £16.00, Children / Full-time Students £4.00
