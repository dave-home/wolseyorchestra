Friends

Amateur orchestras cannot survive without support from regular, enthusiastic audience members. We very much appreciate the help of the Friends of the Wolsey Orchestra, and would also like to thank those who have contributed financially to the orchestra's success.

If you would like to help secure the future if the orchestra, you might consider helping us with one of our specific expenses, or becoming a Friend. In return for a modest contribution of £25, Friends are entitled to a £2 reduction in ticket prices and a free concert programme.

If you would like to support the orchestra, become a 'Friend', or wish to make any other enquiry about supporting the orchestra, please use the feedback form

The Wolsey Orchestra is a member of the Ipswich Arts Association. To find out more about the Association, its members, events and activities, and to read Images magazine online, visit www.ipswich-arts.org.uk

The Wolsey Orchestra is a member of Making Music, formerly the National Federation of Music Societies, which represents and supports amateur choirs, orchestras and music promoters throughout the UK

The Wolsey Orchestra wishes to acknowledge the help we receive from Melody Management in the promotion of our concerts
